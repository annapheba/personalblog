Freezed Frames

"We keep this love in a photograph
We made these memories for ourselves
Where our eyes are never closing
Hearts are never broken
And time's forever frozen, still"

- Photograph by Ed Sheeran

This is where I try to pen down my muddled thoughts and late night musings, you are welcome to read through them!

But why is it muddled, you might be wondering? I write from my experiences, I have a hard time turning my thoughts to words, and it's all muddled in there, you see. I have already used the word muddled like five times already, which is pretty muddled.

I searched for the meaning of "muddled" and they say - not arranged in order; untidy OR Not clear or coherent; confused. Perfecto! It's a mix of both meanings and quite ironical since I hate it when things are not in order and untidy, a slight obsession, some people say!
